About Us

Sayona Drinks was founded in 2005 by a small team of Dar es Salaam entrepreneurs who wanted to make good quality soft drinks affordable for every Tanzanian household. Today we supply shops, supermarkets, and wholesalers across the country.

Our Values

  • Quality – every bottle passes strict taste and safety checks.
  • Affordability – fair prices for every household.
  • Local sourcing – fruits and sugar sourced from Tanzanian farmers.
  • Innovation – new flavours added every year.

Our Journey

2005

Company founded in Dar es Salaam with one production line.

2012

Expanded distribution to Arusha, Mwanza, and Dodoma.

2019

Launched the Sayona Juicy fruit juice line.

2026

Now serving customers in over 15 regions of Tanzania.
